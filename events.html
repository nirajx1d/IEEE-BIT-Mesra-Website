Timeline of Events

C/C++ workshop

25th - 27th August

C/C++ workshop provides coding enthusiasts from the newly admitted batch, an opportunity to come forth and embellish their coding skills. The workshop mainly aims at helping the students with the programming language C/C++. It also introduces young folks to the world of competitive coding.

Cash flow

14th October

Organised in “Pantheon: The annual technical fest of BIT Mesra”,’Cashflow’ is a virtual stock market game for business interested geeks. Learn more about the buy-sell period of the stocks and take a dive into the world of Stock Trading!

A2Z Quiz

15th October

Organised in “Pantheon: The annual technical fest of BIT Mesra”,’A2Z Quiz’ is an open invitation to all kinds of quizzers. The quiz covers up all the topics of a normal human life. Being a general quiz with enthralling questions, it draws great participation from the students.

IEEE day

3rd October

On a day to celebrate innovations, technologies, engineering and some amazing ideas which have capabilities to change the world IEEE Student Branch-BIT Mesra organised an online tech debate, tech talk on IEEE day. Exciting goodies were given to the winners

Digital Electronics workshop

6th - 8th November

This workshop organised in association with ECESOC BIT Mesra.The workshop caters the attention of second year students but the students from the rest of the batches, who are interested to learn are also eligible. Be it designing those cool chips, or making embedded systems or creating a robot, it all starts from here.

Into the future

24th - 31st December

Into the future’ is an online event where people share amazing and out of the box ideas of technologies of the future. The main aim behind this event is to actually know what we, the to-be engineers, have in mind about future technology and create a short summary which can be beneficial in many ways. Judgment is based on how implementable, pragmatic, unique and useful the idea is.

IOT workshop

20th - 21st January

Internet of Things no more needs any introduction. To spread awareness and cognizance about this aspect of internet which excels in both novelty and utility, IEEE BIT Mesra in association with i3indiya Technologies conducts this workshop which draws massive participation from students at the college.

Create.Code.Do

12th February

IEEE Student Chapter BIT Mesra in association with WeCP - We Create Problems conducted a competition CREATE.CODE.DO where participants created problems. The winners received cash prizes and a direct written test for internship with the organisation.

Da Vinci code

15th February

Organised in “Bitotsav: The annual cultural fest of BIT Mesra”,’The Da Vinci Code’ is a code deciphering game in which the participants solve puzzles and problems. The first round is a MCQ based general aptitude leading up to the final 4th round where participants have to reach the destination by cracking the destination clue.

Up in the air

16th February

Organised in “Bitotsav: The annual cultural fest of BIT Mesra”,’Up in the air’ is a fun filled design-based event where the participants explore the mysteries of flight and create a device from the raw materials provided that can remain airborne to cover the maximum distance.

Data Structures workshop

20th - 22nd March

Organised around march every year the “Data Structures Workshop” focuses on the ‘fundamental concepts of Data Structure: Their uses and applications. The workshop helps in simplifying and enhancing the user’s capability of effective code execution. The topics covered are Linked List, Stack, Queues, Graphs, Trees, etc. The presentations provided for further revision help the students a great deal.

Membership Awareness Programme

8th April

The seminar was graced by the presence of Mr. Shivam Abhilash, Technical Co-ordinator (Kolkata Region) , IEEE India Computer Society India Council SAC. The purpose of the seminar was to make the students aware on the future opportunities for them in the college, the IEEE Global Membership and it’s benefits.
